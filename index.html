What we are about?

Our Mission

Our mission as the Bread of Life Church of the Nazarene (COTN) is to make Christlike disciples in all nations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to advance God's kingdom by: Preaching about God's holy scripture, conversion of sinners, the entire sanctification of believers and simplicity of living a holy life depending upon the Holy Spirit.

Our Belief

We believe in the Great Commission for "the harvest is plentiful but the workers are few" (Matt 9:47).
