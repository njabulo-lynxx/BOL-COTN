JOHN 17

According to John chapter 17: verse 1, what did Jesus do after he said this?

  1. He looked toward heaven and prayed (17:1).

Who is the only true God?

  1. The Father (17:3).

What did Jesus have with the Father before the world began?

  1. Glory (17:5).

What did the disciples know with certainty?

  1. That Jesus came from the Father (17:8).

Whose is all that the Father has?

  1. Jesus' (17:10).

According to John chapter 17: verse 12, what did Jesus do while he was still with his disciples?

  1. Protected them and kept them safe by that name the Father gave Jesus (17:12).

Who has hated the disciples?

  1. The world (17:14).

According to John chapter 17: verse 16, how are the disciples not of the world?

  1. Even as Jesus is not of it (17:16).

How has Jesus sent the disciples into the world?

  1. As the Father sent him into the world (17:18).

For whom does Jesus also pray?

  1. For those who will believe in Jesus through the disciples' message (17:20).

Why has Jesus given the believers the glory that the Father gave him?

  1. That they may be one as Jesus and the Father are one (17:22).

According to John chapter 17: verse 25, whom does the world not know?

  1. The Righteous Father (17:25).

What will Jesus continue to do?

  1. Make the Father known in order that the love the Father has for Jesus may be in the believers and that Jesus himself may be in them (17:26).
