JOHN 18

Who knew the place, because Jesus had often met there with his disciples?

  1. Judas, who betrayed Jesus (18:2).

Who were carrying torches, lanterns and weapons?

  1. A detachment of soldiers and some officials from the chief priests and Pharisees (who were guided to the olive grove by Judas). (18:3).

Who came to the olive grove?

  1. Judas, a detachment of soldiers, and some officials from the chief priests and Pharisees (18:3).

Situation question who asked it again, of whom, and what was the reply: "Who is it you want?"

  1. Jesus asked it of the detachment of soldiers and some officials from the chief priests and the Pharisees and they said, "Jesus of Nazareth" (18:7).

What did Simon Peter draw?

  1. A sword (18:10).

Who was the high priest that year?

  1. Caiaphas (18:13).

Who was known to the high priest?

  1. Another disciple who was following Jesus with Simon Peter (18:15).

What did the girl at the door ask Peter?

  1. "You are not one of his disciples, are you?" (18:17).

What did the servants and officials do to keep warm?

  1. Stood around a fire they had made (18 18).

Who surely know what Jesus said?

  1. Those who heard him (18.21).

What did one of the officials nearby demand?

  1. "Is this the way you answer the high priest?" (18:22).

To whom did Annas send Jesus?

  1. Caiaphas the high priest (18:24).

Who challenged Peter?

  1. One of the high priest's servants, a relative of the man whose car Peter had cut off (18:26).

To avoid what did the Jews not enter the palace?

  1. Ceremonial uncleanness (18:28).

Three part answer, In John Chapter 18, to what three people did the Jews take Jesus?

  1. 1) Annas (18:13). 2) Caiaphas the high priest (18:24). 3) Pilate (18:29)

Who have no right to execute anyone?

  1. The Jews (18:31).

When did Pilate summon Jesus and ask him, "Are you the king of the Jews?"

  1. When he went back inside the palace (18:33).

What if Jesus' kingdom were of this world?

  1. Jesus' servants would fight to prevent his arrest by the Jews (18:36).

According to John chapter 18 verse 37, who listens to Jesus?

  1. Everyone on the side of truth (18:37)

Who asked, "What is truth?"

  1. Pilate (18:38).

What is the Jew's custom?

  1. For Pilate to relcase to them one prisoner at the time of the Passover (18:39).

What did the Jews shout back?

  1. "No, not him! Give us Barabbas!" (18:40).
