JOHN 21

From where was Nathanael?

  1. Cana in Galilee (21:2).

When did the disciples not realize that it was Jesus?

  1. Early in the morning when they saw Jesus standing on the shore (21:4).

According to John chapter 21: versc 6, what did Jesus say?

  1. "Throw your net on the right side of the boat and you will find some" (21:6).

Who jumped into the water?

  1. Simon Peter (21:7).

Into what did Simon Peter jump?

  1. The water (21:7).

Who climbed aboard and dragged the net ashore?

  1. Simon Peter (21:11).

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the response: "Come and have breakfast"?

  1. Jesus said it to the disciples and none of them dared ask him, "Who are you?" (21:12).

When did Jesus say to Simon Peter, "Simon son of John, do you truly love me more than these?"

  1. When they had finished cating (21:15).

Why was Peter hurt?

  1. Because Jesus asked him the third time, "Do you love me?" (21:17).

When did Peter go where he wanted?

  1. When he was younger (21:18).

Whom did Peter turn and see was following Jesus and him?

  1. The disciple whom Jesus loved (21:20).

What if Jesus wants the disciple whom he loved to remain alive until he returns?

  1. What is that to Peter? (21:22).

What spread among the brothers?

  1. The rumor that the disciple whom Jesus loved would not die (21:23).

Who testifies to these things and wrote them down?

  1. The disciple whom Jesus loved (21:24).

What if every one of the many other things Jesus did were written down?

  1. Even the whole world would not have room for the books that would be written (21:25).
