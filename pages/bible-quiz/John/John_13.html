JOHN 13

What had the devil already done?

  1. Prompted Judas Iscariot, son of Simon, to betray Jesus (13:2).

According to John chapter 13: verse 4, what did Jesus take off?

  1. His outer clothing (13:4).

When will Simon Peter understand?

  1. Later (13:7)

What does a person who has had a bath need only to wash?

  1. His feet (13:10).

Whose feet should the disciples also wash?

  1. One another's (13:14).

According to John chapter 13 verse 17, what if the disciples do these things?

  1. They will be blessed (13:17).

Who accepts Jesus?

  1. Whoever accepts anyone Jesus sends (13:20).

Who was reclining next to Jesus?

  1. The disciple whom Jesus loved (13:23).

Situation question who said it, to whom, and when "Lord, who is it?"

  1. The disciple whom Jesus loved said it to Jesus when Simon Peter motioned to this disciple and said, "Ask him which one he means" (13:24, 25)

When did Satan enter into Judas?

  1. As soon as Judas took the bread (13-27)

For what did some think Jesus was telling Judas to buy what was needed?

  1. The Feast (13:29).

Who is now glorified?

  1. The Son of Man (13:31).

What does Jesus tell his disciples now, just as he told the Jews?

  1. Where he is going, they cannot come (13:33)

What will all men know if the disciples love one another?

  1. That they are Jesus' disciples (13:35)

Who will disown Jesus three times before the rooster crows?

  1. Peter (13:38)

Complete, in essence, the verse from which the following unique word comes: "Disown"?

  1. "Then Jesus answered, "Will you really lay down your life for me? I tell you the truth, before the rooster crows, you will disown me three times!" (13:38)
