JOHN 6

According to John chapter 6. verse 1, to where did Jesus cross some time after this?

  1. To the far shore of the Sea of Galilee (that is, the Sea of Tiberius) (6:1).

Where did Jesus sit down with his disciples?

  1. On a mountainside (6:3).

Situation question who said it, to whom, and in what situation: "Where shall we buy bread for these people to eat?"

  1. Jesus said it to Philip when he looked up and saw a great crowd coming toward him (6:5)

Who was Simon Peter's brother?

  1. Andrew (6:8).

What should the disciples let be wasted?

  1. Nothing (6:12).

What did the people intend to do by force?

  1. Come and make Jesus king (6:15).

How was Jesus approaching the boat?

  1. Walking on the water (6:19)

When did the boat immediately reach the shore where the disciples were heading?

  1. When the disciples were willing to take Jesus into the boat (6:21).

What did the crowd do once they realized that neither Jesus nor his disciples were there?

  1. They got into the boats and went to Capernaum in search of Jesus (6:24).

Who will give the crowd food that endures to eternal life?

  1. The Son of Man (6:27).

What did our forefathers eat in the desert?

  1. The manna (6:31).

Who is the bread of life?

  1. Jesus (6:35, 48).

According to John chapter 6. verse 37, what will come to Jesus?

  1. All that the Father gives him (6.37).

Who began to grumble about Jesus?

  1. The Jews (6:41).

By whom will they all be taught?

  1. God (6:45).

Who has everlasting life?

  1. He who believes (6:47).

What will Jesus give for the life of the world?

  1. His flesh (6:51).

What does whoever cats Jesus' flesh and drinks his blood have?

  1. Eternal life (6:54).

Who will live because of Jesus?

  1. The one who feeds on him (6:57)?

Who were grumbling about Jesus' teaching?

  1. Many of his disciples (6:61).

Who gives life?

  1. The Spirit (6:63).

What did Jesus go on to say?

  1. "This is why I told you that no one can come to me unless the Father has enabled him" (6:65).

Who has the words of eternal life?

  1. Jesus (6:68).

Who is a devil?

  1. One of the Twelve (6:70).

Who was the son of Simon Iscariot?

  1. Judas (6:71).
