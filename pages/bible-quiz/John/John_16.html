JOHN 16

Out of what will the world put the disciples?

  1. Out of the synagogue (16:2).

According to John chapter 16: verse 4, what will the disciples remember when the time comes?

  1. That Jesus warned them (16:4).

Why are Jesus' disciples filled with grief?

  1. Because Jesus has said these things (16:6).

Two part answer: when will the Counselor not come to the disciples and when will Jesus send him to them?

  1. Unless Jesus goes away the Counselor will not come to the disciples, but if he goes, he will send him to them (16:7).

Who now stands condemned?

  1. The prince of this world (16:11).

How much more docs Jesus have to say to the disciples?

  1. More than they can now bear (16:12).

Why did Jesus say the Spirit of truth will take from what is his and make it known to the disciples?

  1. Because all that belongs to the Father is his (Jesus'). (16:15).

According to John chapter 16: verse 16, when will the disciples see Jesus?

  1. After a little while (16:16).

Situation question: who said it, and to whom: "What does he mean by saying, 'In a little while you will see me no more, and then after a little while you will see me' and 'Because 1 am going to the Father"?

  1. Some of Jesus' disciples said it to one another (16:17).

What will turn to joy?

  1. The disciples' grief (16:20).

Who forgets the anguish because of her joy?

  1. A woman giving birth to a child when her baby is born (16:21).

According to John chapter 16: verse 23, what will Jesus' Father give the disciples?

  1. Whatever they ask in Jesus' name (16:23).

Who loves the disciples because they have loved Jesus?

  1. The Father himself (16:27).

What does Jesus not even need?

  1. To have anyone ask him questions (16:30).

Who has overcome the world?

  1. Jesus (16:33).
