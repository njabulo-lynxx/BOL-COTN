JOHN 11

What was the village of Mary and her sister Martha?

  1. Bethany (11:1).

What will not end in death?

  1. Lazarus' sickness (11:4).

When did Jesus stay where he was two more days?

  1. When he heard that Lazarus was sick (11:6).

According to John chapter 11: verse 8, who tried to stone Jesus a short time ago?

  1. The Jews (11:8).

Who has fallen asleep?

  1. Lazarus (11:11).

What did Jesus tell his disciples plainly?

  1. "Lazarus is dead" (11:14).

How long did Jesus find that Lazarus had already been in the tomb?

  1. Four days (11:17).

What was less than two miles from Jerusalem?

  1. Bethany (11:18).

Who stayed at home?

  1. Mary (11:20).

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the reply. "Your brother will rise again"?

  1. Jesus said it to Martha, and she answered, "I know he will rise again in the resurrection at the last day" (11:23, 24).

Who was to come into the world?

  1. The Christ, the Son of God (11:27).

Who had not yet entered the village, but was still at the place where Martha had met him?

  1. Jesus (11:30).

When was Jesus troubled?

  1. When he saw Mary weeping and the Jews who had come along with her also weeping (11:33).

who wept?

  1. Jesus (11:35).

What was laid across the entrance?

  1. A stone (11:38).

Why does Jesus thank his Father?

  1. That he has heard him (11:41).

What was around the dead man's face?

  1. A cloth (11:44).

Who called a meeting of the Sanhedrin?

  1. The chief priests and the Pharisees (11:47).

Who prophesied that Jesus would die for the Jewish nation?

  1. Caiaphas (11:51).

Why did Jesus no longer move about publicly among the Jews?

  1. Because from that day on the Sanhedrin plotted to take his life (11:53, 54).

Who had given orders that if anyone found out where Jesus was, he should report it so that they might arrest him?

  1. The chief priests and Pharisees (11:57).
