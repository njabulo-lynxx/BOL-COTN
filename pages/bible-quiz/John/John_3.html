JOHN 3

Who was a member of the Jewish ruling council?

  1. Nicodemus (a man of the Pharisees) (3:1).

What can a man surely not do a second time?

  1. Enter into his mother's womb to be born (3:4).

According to John chapter 3: verse 5, unless what can no one enter the kingdom of God?

  1. Unless he is born of water and the Spirit (3:5).

What blows wherever it pleases?

  1. The wind (3:8).

From where did the Son of Man come?

  1. From heaven (3:13).

Who shall not perish?

  1. Whoever believes in God's one and only Son (3:16).

Who stands condemned already?

  1. Whoever does not believe in God's Son (3:18)?

Into what does whoever lives by the truth come?

  1. The light (3:21).

Who was put in prison?

  1. John (3:24).

Who said, "I am not the Christ but am sent ahead of him"?

  1. John (3:28).

According to John chapter 3: versc 30, what must John become?

  1. Less (3:30).

Without what does God give the Spirit?

  1. Limit (3:34).

On whom does God's wrath remain?

  1. On whomever rejects the Son (3:36).
