JOHN 9

What happened so that the work of God might be displayed in the life of the man born blind?

  1. He was born blind (9:3).

Where did Jesus spit?

  1. On the ground (9:6).

According to John chapter 9: verse 8, what did those who had formerly seen the man begging ask?

  1. "Isn't this the same man who used to sit and beg?" (9:8).

What was the day on which Jesus had made mud and opened the man's eyes?

  1. A Sabbath (9:14).

Why did some of the Pharisees say Jesus was not from God?

  1. For he did not keep the Sabbath (9:16).

Who sent for the man's parents?

  1. The Jews (9:18).

Who is of age?

  1. The man who had been borm blind (9:21)

Situation question: to whom was it said, when, and what was the reply: "Give glory to God, we know this man is a sinner"?

  1. It was said to the man who had been blind when the Pharisees (or Jews) summoned him a second time. He replied, "Whether he is a sinner or not, I don't know. One thing I do know. I was blind but now I soc!" (9.24-25).

To whom does God not listen?

  1. To sinners (9:31).

Who was steeped in sin at birth?

  1. The man who had been blind (9:34).

Whom did Jesus say the man has now seen?

  1. The Son of Man (9:37)

Who will become blind?

  1. Those who see (9:39).

What if some Pharisees were blind?

  1. They would not be guilty of sin (9:41).
