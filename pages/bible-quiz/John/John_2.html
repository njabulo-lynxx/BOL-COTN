JOHN 2

What took place on the third day?

  1. A wedding (2:1).

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and when: "They have no more wine"?

  1. Jesus' mother said it to Jesus when the wine was gone (2:3).

What did the servants fill to the brim?

  1. The six stone water jars (2:7).

What did Jesus perform at Cana in Galilee?

  1. The first of his miraculous signs (turning the water into winc). (2:11).

Who went up to Jerusalem when it was almost time for the Jewish Passover?

  1. Jesus (2:13).

Who overturned the tables of the moncy changers?

  1. Jesus (2:15).

Complete, in essence, the verse from which the following unique word comes: "Zeal"?

  1. "His disciples remembered that it is written: 'Zeal for your house will consume me.'" (2:17).

What will Jesus raise again in three days?

  1. This temple (his body). (2:19).

Who then believed the Scripture and the words that Jesus had spoken?

  1. His disciples (2:22).

According to John chapter 2: verse 24, whom did Jesus know?

  1. All men (2:24).
