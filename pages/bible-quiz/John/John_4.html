JOHN 4

When did the Lord leave Judca and go back once more to Galilee?

  1. When he learned that the Pharisees heard that he was gaining and baptizing more disciples than John (4:3).

What is called Sychar?

  1. A town in Samaria (4:5).

According to John chapter 4: verse 7, what did Jesus say to the Samaritan woman?

  1. "Will you give me a drink?" (4:7).

What is deep?

  1. The well (Jacob's well). (4:11).

Who will never thirst?

  1. Whoever drinks the water Jesus gives him (4:14).

According to John chapter 4: verse 17, when is the Samaritan woman right?

  1. When she says she has no husband (4:17).

Who worship what they know?

  1. The Jews (4:22).

When will Messiah explain everything to the Samaritans?

  1. When he comes (4:25).

Who left her water jar?

  1. The Samaritan woman (4:28).

What did Jesus' disciples urge him?

  1. "Rabbi, cat something" (4:31).

According to John chapter 4: verse 33, what did his disciples say to each other?

  1. "Could someone have brought him food?" (4:33).

Who even now draws his wages?

  1. The reaper (4:36).

How long did Jesus stay with the Samaritans?

  1. Two days (4:40)

Who has no honor in his own country?

  1. A prophet (4:44).

Who was close to death?

  1. A certain royal official's son (4:47).

Who took Jesus at his word and departed?

  1. The royal official (4:50).

According to John chapter 4: verse 54, what was this?

  1. The second miraculous sign that Jesus performed (4:54).
