John 12

How many days before the Passover did Jesus arrive at Bethany?

  1. Six (12:1).

What was worth a year's wages?

  1. The perfume Mary poured on Jesus' feet (12:5).

For what was it intended that Mary should save this perfume?

  1. For the day of Jesus' burial (12:7).

Who made plans to kill Lazarus as well?

  1. The chief priests (12:10).

Who heard that Jesus was on his way to Jerusalem?

  1. The great crowd that had come for the feast (12:12).

Who is seated on a donkey's colt?

  1. The Daughter of Zion's king (12:15).

According to John chapter 12: verse 18, what had many people heard?

  1. That Jesus had given this miraculous sign (12:18).

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the result: "Sir, we would like to see Jesus"?

  1. Some Grocks who were among those who went up to worship at the Feast said it to Philip, Philip went to tell Andrew, and they in turn told Jesus (12:20-22).

What if a kernel of wheat dies?

  1. It produces many seeds (12:24).

What is troubled now?

  1. Jesus' heart (12:27).

Who will be driven out?

  1. The prince of this world (12:31).

What has the crowd heard from the Law?

  1. That the Christ will remain forever (12.34).

What was to fulfill the word of Isaiah the prophet?

  1. Even after Jesus had done these miraculous signs in the crowd's presence, they did still not believe in Jesus (12:37-8).

Who saw Jesus' glory and spoke about him?

  1. Isaiah (12:41).

Who does not believe in Jesus only, but in the one who sent him?

  1. A man who believes in Jesus (12:44).

According to John chapter 12: verse 47, whom does Jesus not judge?

  1. The person who hears Jesus' words but does not keep them (12:47)

What leads to eternal life?

  1. The Father's command (12:50).
