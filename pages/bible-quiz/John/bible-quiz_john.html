Bible Quizzing for Youth

Bible Study and Practice/Competition Questions for the Book of John

Rules:

  1. 5 participants per team
  2. 50 questions - 50 points
  3. 20 multiple choice questions - 20 points
  4. 5 memory verses - 30 points
  5. Each team member 10 questions of the 50
  6. Each team member 4 questions of the multiple choice
  7. 3 Randomly chosen team members to do 1 randomly chosen memory verse

All scores will count for the church represented by the quizer. Also the highest scorer as individual quizer will be awarded a prize

Memory verses:

  1. JOHN 1;1-12
  2. JOHN 3; 1-10 OR JOHN 3: 16-21
  3. JOHN 10:7-18
  4. JOHN 14:6-14 OR JOHN: 14: 15-21 OR JOHN 14: 23-31
  5. JOHN 15: 1-14
  6. JOHN 17: 20-26

With memory verses 2 and 4 the quizzer will have the choice which verse they will say
