JOHN 10

Who enters by the gate?

  1. The man who is the shepherd of his sheep (10:2)

Whose voice do the sheep not recognize?

  1. A stranger's (10:5)

Who were thieves and robbers?

  1. All who ever came before Jesus (10:8)

Complete, in essence, the verse from which the following unique word comes: "Pasture"?

  1. "I am the gate, whoever enters through me will be saved. He will come in and go out, and find pasture" (10:9).

Who is not the shepherd who owns the sheep?

  1. The hired hand (10:12)

Who are not of this shoop pen?

  1. Other shoep Jesus has (10:16)

Who were again divided at these words?

  1. The Jews (10:19)

Where was Jesus walking?

  1. In Solomon's Colonnade (10:23)

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and in what situation: "How long will you keep us in suspense? If you are the Christ, tell us plainly"

  1. The Jews said it to Jesus when they gathered around him while he was walking in the temple area in Solomon's Colonnade during the Feast of Dedication at Jerusalem (10:24)

Who shall never perish?

  1. Jesus' sheep (10:28)

What has Jesus shown the Jews?

  1. Many great miracles from the Father (10:32)

According to John chapter 10: verse 34, what is written in the Jews' Law?

  1. "I have said you are gods" (10:34)

Who escaped the Jews' grasp?

  1. Jesus (10:39)

According to John chapter 10: verse 42, who believed in Jesus?

  1. Many (in that place) (10:42)
