JOHN 5

What is surrounded by five coverod colonnades?

  1. A pool-which in Aramaic is called Bethesda-in Jerusalem near the Sheep Gato (5:2).

According to John chapter 5: verse 6, what did Jesus Icarn?

  1. That the invalid had been in this condition for a long time (5:6).

When does the invalid have no one to help him into the pool?

  1. When the water is stirred (5:7).

What does the law forbid?

  1. Carrying your mat on the Sabbath (5:10).

Where did Jesus later find the man who was healed?

  1. At the temple (5:14).

Who persecuted Jesus?

  1. The Jews (5:16).

Why can the Son do only what he sees his Father doing?

  1. Because whatever the Father docs the Son also does (5:19).

Whom does the Father judge?

  1. No one (5:22).

Whose voice will the dead hear?

  1. The Son of God (5:25).

What will all who are in their graves hear?

  1. The Son's voice (5:28).

According to John chapter 5: verse 30, how can Jesus do nothing?

  1. By himself (5:30).

Who was a lamp that burned and gave light?

  1. John (5:35).

Two part question: 1) what have the Jews never heard, and 2) what have the Jews never seen?

  1. 1) The Father's voice and 2) The Father's form (5:37).

From whom does Jesus not accept praise?

  1. From men (5:41).

In whose name has Jesus come?

  1. In his Father's name (5:43).
