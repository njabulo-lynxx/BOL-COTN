JOHN 15

Who is the true vine?

  1. Jesus (15:1).

What does the gardener prune?

  1. Every branch that does bear fruit (15.2).

What can no branch bear by itself?

  1. Fruit (15:4).

Who is like a branch that is thrown away and withers?

  1. Anyone who does not remain in Jesus (15:6).

How has Jesus loved the disciples?

  1. As the Father has loved him (15:9).

What may be complete?

  1. The disciples' joy (15:11).

What if the disciples do what Jesus commands?

  1. They are his friends (15:14).

What does Jesus no longer call the disciples?

  1. Servants (15:15).

According to John chapter 15: verse 16, whom did Jesus choose?

  1. The disciples (15:16).

What fruit did Jesus appoint the disciples to bear?

  1. Fruit that will last (15:16).

Out of what has Jesus chosen the disciples?

  1. The world (15:19).

What if the world obeyed Jesus' teaching?

  1. They will obey the disciples' also (15:20).

Who hates Jesus' Father as well?

  1. He who hates Jesus (15:23).

Who goes out from the Father?

  1. The Spirit of truth (15:26).

Why must the disciples also testify?

  1. For they have been with Jesus from the beginning (15:27).
