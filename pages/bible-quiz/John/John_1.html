JOHN 1

Who was in the beginning?

  1. The Word (1:1).

What has the darkness not understood?

  1. The light (which shines in the darkness) (1:5).

Why did John come as a witness?

  1. To testify concerning that light (so that through him all men might believe). (1:7).

Whom did the world not recognize?

  1. The true light [OR Jesus, the Word] (1:10).

According to John chapter 1: verse 12, to whom did he give the right to become children of God?

  1. To all who received him, to those who believed in his name (1:12).

Three part answer. Of what are children of God not born?

  1. Natural descent, human decision, or a husband's will (1:13).

What did the Word become?

  1. Flesh (1:14).

Through whom was the law given?

  1. Moses (1:17)

According to John chapter 1: verse 21, whom did John say he was not?

  1. Elijah or the Prophet (1:21).

With what does John baptize?

  1. With water (1:26).

According to John chapter 1: verse 28, where did all this happen?

  1. At Bethany on the other side of the Jordan, where John was baptizing (1:28).

Who came down from heaven as a dove and remained on Jesus?

  1. The Spirit (1:32).

According to John chapter 1: verse 39, about what hour was it?

  1. About the tenth hour (1:39).

Whose brother was Andrew?

  1. Simon Peter's (1:40).

From where was Philip?

  1. The town of Bethsaida (1:44).

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the reply: "We have found the one Moses wrote about in the Law, and about whom the prophets also wrote--Jesus of Nazareth, the son of Joseph"?

  1. Philip said it to Nathanael and he replied, "Nazareth! Can anything good come from there?" (1:45-46).

Who is a true Israelite?

  1. Nathanael (1:47).

On whom shall the angels of God ascend and descend?

  1. The Son of Man (1:51).
