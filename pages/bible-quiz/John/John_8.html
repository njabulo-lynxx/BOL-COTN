JOHN 8

When did all the people gather around Jesus?

  1. At dawn (8:2).

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and in what situation: "Teacher, this woman was caught in the act of adultery"?

  1. The teachers of the law and the Pharisees said it to Jesus when they brought in a woman caught in adultery and made her stand before the group (8:4).

What did Jesus straighten up and say to the teachers of the law and the Pharisees?

  1. "If any one of you is without sin, let him be the first to throw a stone at her" (8:7)

What did the older ones do?

  1. Went away first (one at a time). (8:9).

According to John chapter 8: verse 12, what is Jesus?

  1. The light of the world (8:12).

Why did the Pharisees say Jesus' testimony is not valid?

  1. Because he is appcaring as his own witness (8:13).

When are Jesus' decisions right?

  1. If he does judge (8:16).

What if the Pharisecs know Jesus?

  1. They would know his Father also (8:19).

What did this make the Jews ask?

  1. "Will he kill himself? Is that why he says, 'Where I go, you cannot come?" (8:22).

Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the reply: "Who are you?"

  1. The Jews said it to Jesus and he replied, "Just what I have been claiming all along" (8:25).

When will the Jews know that Jesus is the one he claims to be?

  1. When they have lifted up the Son of Man (8:28).

When are the Jews really Jesus' disciples?

  1. If they hold to his teaching (8:31).

What is everyone who sins?

  1. A slave to sin (8:34).

Why are Abraham's descendants ready to kill Jesus?

  1. Because they have no room for his word (8:37).

According to John chapter 8: verse 41, what are the Jews doing?

  1. The things their own father does (8:41).

Whose desire do the Jews want to carry out?

  1. The devil's (who is their father). (8:44).

By whom is Jesus not possessed?

  1. A demon (8:49).

When does Jesus' glory mean nothing?

  1. If he glorifics himself (8:54).

Who picked up stones to stone Jesus?

  1. The Jews (8:59).
