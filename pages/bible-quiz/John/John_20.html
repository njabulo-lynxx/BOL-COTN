JOHN 20

According to John chapter 20: verse 1, what had been removed from the entrance?

  1. The stone (20:1)

Who reached the tomb first?

  1. The other disciple (20:4).

Who bent over and looked in the tomb?

  1. The other disciple (20:5).

What had been around Jesus' head?

  1. The burial cloth (20:7).

Who stood outside the tomb crying?

  1. Mary (Magdalene). (20:11).

What did two angels in white ask Mary?

  1. "Woman, why are you crying?" (20:13).

Situation question who said it, to whom, and what was she thinking: "Sir, if you have carried him away, tell me where you have put him, and I will get him"?

  1. Mary said it to Jesus, thinking he was the gardener (20:15).

What did Mary cry out in Aramaic?

  1. To "Rabboni!" (20:16)

With what news did Mary Magdalene go to the disciples?

  1. "I have seen the Lord!" (20:18).

When were the disciples overjoyed?

  1. When they saw the Lord (20:20)

In John chapter 20, how did the disciples receive the Holy Spirit?

  1. Jesus breathed on them and said, "Receive the Holy Spirit" (20:22).

Who was not with the disciples when Jesus came?

  1. Thomas (called Didymus). (20:24).

Unless what will Thomas not believe it?

  1. Unless he sees the nail marks in Jesus hands and puts his finger where the nails were, and put his hand into his side (20:25).

Who said, "My Lord and my God!"?

  1. Thomas (20:28).

Where did Jesus do many other miraculous signs?

  1. In the presence of his disciples (20:30)

What may you have by believing?

  1. Life in Jesus' name (20:31).
